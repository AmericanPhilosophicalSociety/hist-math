About the Gallery

Contours of American Mathematics was completed to coincide with the 2026 International Congress of Mathematicians, held in Philadelphia. Karen Hunger Parshall served as the project's curator. The project website was designed by David Ragnar Nelson. Adrianna Link and Bayard L. Miller provided curatorial assistance and, along with Catherine Person and Ali Rospond, helped revise the text and interface. Sabrina Bocangera digitized the objects featured in this gallery.

This website was built with the static site generator Hugo and is released open source. It makes use of Hugo Bibliography, a community project sponsored by DHTech.
